✏️ How to write a good prompt

  • Describe one scene — the model works best with a single continuous shot.
  • Include lighting & mood: golden hour, neon lights, foggy, sunny, dramatic.
  • Add camera style: cinematic, slow motion, aerial view, close-up.
  • Mention motion: waves crashing, car speeding, leaves blowing in wind.
